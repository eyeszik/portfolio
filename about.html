ABOUT
Isaac Bishop - DIGITAL DESIGNS

I’m a multidisciplinary designer who lives in California. I create holistic digital aesthetics that translate across platforms. I have over 10 years of industry experience working with clients from all types of industries. I design web & mobile user-interface page layouts, creating the user experience and the interactions of digital content, Video & Motion Graphics, Front-End Web Development, and designing content for Digital & Traditional Marketing material. Everything that I create is for a brand or business to stand out from its competitors. I'm in the service of helping businesses grow and to be seen.

I apply interactive and visual design principles on websites and web applications for a positive and cohesive user experience. I ensure that all elements of the online user experience are optimized for improved usability, usefulness, and exceptional visual design.
